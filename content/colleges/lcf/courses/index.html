If you want to study at London College of Fashion but find it hard to commit to a full time course, then you could think about studying flexibly. We have carefully developed undergraduate and postgraduate courses to meet the challenges of the fashion industry.

Our part-time courses will develop a sound knowledge of the chosen subject combined with a realistic and practical understanding of the demands of a specialist industry sector. There are few opportunities nationally to study fashion on a part-time basis, and these courses respond to the needs of a variety of students who are unable to engage in full-time study or who prefer to study part-time.

Undergraduate courses

We have four undergraduate BA (Hons) available to study part-time over 4 years:

  • BA (Hons) Fashion Design (Part time)

    Exploring the creative, technical, cultural and economic aspects of the fashion process.

  • BA (Hons) Fashion Business (Part time)

    A part-time credit-based course for professionals seeking careers in all areas of fashion industry management.

  • BA (Hons) Fashion Media (Part time)

    Exploring the cultural and media contexts of the fashion process.

  • BA (Hons) Fashion Visual Merchandising and Branding (Part time)

    Flexible learning course in fashion brand management and window display

Masters courses

All Masters courses at London College of Fashion are available to study part-time over 30 months (8 terms).
