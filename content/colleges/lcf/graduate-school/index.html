Graduate
School

The Graduate School at LCF brings together the next generation of fashion talent.

With a unique focus on research, innovation and collaboration, our dynamic range of courses aim to fuel, equip and inspire. Recruited from a diverse range of backgrounds and disciplines, our graduates are shaping all areas of the industry at a global level - from culture and communication to fashion and design. Whether you are looking to make creative connections, hone your craft or challenge your thinking, the Graduate School at LCF is leading the way.
