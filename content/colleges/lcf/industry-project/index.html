What was the inspiration for your collection?

The inspiration behind my designs comes from childhood fantasy, family and home. I like to experiment with everything I design and make. I create something new and exciting whilst also expressing myself as an artist. In this collection I have used wadding and sheep skin, and balanced it against loud digital prints. The collection is styled with loud accessories which accentuates the sense of fun. I have used perfectly dyed fur and mad eccentric colours. I wanted to create an eye-catching collection, which is also elegant. 

What are your plans for the future? 

I am influenced by JW Anderson and Christopher Kane, and my dream job would to be work with high-end womenswear brands.
