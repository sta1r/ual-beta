Powering the fashion industry

Our graduates are running the international fashion industry. They hold influential positions ranging from editors, content makers and fashion designers, to brand directors and true business entrepreneurs. 

This reflects our commitment to embedding industry experience across our courses. We give students opportunities to work with some of the most relevant, challenging and famous companies in the world.
