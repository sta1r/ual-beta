Student Enterprise and Employability (SEE) provides and promotes work opportunities through a number of channels and programmes for University of the Arts London students and graduates, from external paid jobs and internships to internal temporary and commissioned freelance work.

UAL's Creative Opportunities jobs and internships website, offers hundreds of paid work and internship opportunities each month to our in-house temp agency ArtsTemps, which places thousands of UAL students and graduates in a variety of temporary roles across the University.

There are many opportunities to find work both while you are studying and after graduation. SEE also runs internship schemes for UAL graduates, offering high quality, paid and valuable internship experiences within the creative industries.
