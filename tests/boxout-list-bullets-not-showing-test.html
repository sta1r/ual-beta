  • London College of Fashion

The London College of Fashion Library, housed at John Prince's Street, is the UK's leading fashion library.

  • View our opening hours and contact details

Our extensive resources include:

  • 70,000 books on all aspects of the fashion industry from garment construction to cosmetic science.
  • Unique collections of information on fashion designers.
  • Image resources and illustrations.
  • Fashion forecasts
  • DVDs and videos
  • Over 390 print periodicals and 20,000 e-journals
  • Electronic information resources can be accessed via the e-library

Visitors

Visitors may use the library by appointment. Please book in advance by telephone 020 7514 7455/6150 - daily visitor numbers are limited.

Alumni

The library is open to graduates of University of the Arts London who are members of the Alumni Association. Read more UAL Alumni Association benefits.

Subject areas include:

  • Business and management
  • Clothing technology
  • Cosmetic science
  • Costume history
  • Fashion design
  • Footwear and accessories
  • Journalism and media studies
  • Tailoring
  • Textiles
