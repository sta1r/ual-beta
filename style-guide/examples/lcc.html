  • Be the future generation of the creative industries

    London College of Communication is a pioneering world leader in design and media education.

    With the communication sector evolving fast, we work at the cutting edge of new thinking so our students are ready to launch their careers and define the future of the creative industries.

    From Neville Brody to Rankin and from Jefferson Hack to John Hegarty, our graduates go on to shape creative practice across the world, winning awards in their respective fields.

Staff

JOEL KARAMATH

Joel Karamath is Course Leader for BA (Hons) Design for Interaction and Moving Image. He has worked with renowned art institutions including the Geffrye Museum and Victoria and Albert Museum (V&A), commissioned as a consultant and to collaborate on film, layout and exhibition design.

Alumni

TOM HUNTER

Internationally renowned photographer and artist Tom Hunter graduated from LCP’s BA (Hons) Photography course in 1994. Since then he has picked up numerous awards and is the only artist to have a photography show at the National Gallery.
